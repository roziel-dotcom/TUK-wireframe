Before you reserve

The straight answers.

£220 — is it actually worth it? +

One carrier, 4 to 36 months — instead of buying two or three as your baby grows. Set that against a course of physio for the back pain a badly-fitting carrier gives you, and the maths gets simple. Founding-run pricing is below the £220 RRP.

Will it actually fit me? +

That's the whole point. The patent-pending hip belt is engineered for the male pelvis, and the straps are shaped around male shoulders. We're testing on real dads now, across different builds — and founding dads see the results first. There'll be a proper sizing guide at launch, and a 30-day returns window behind it. You've probably been burned before, so we'll show you the proof, not just claim it.

What's the actual spec? +

Model TUKO-BC1. One carrier, 5.5–15 kg (roughly 4 to 36 months), across three carry positions:

  • Front carry, inward facing — from 5.5 kg (~4 months) to 15 kg.
  • Front carry, outward facing — 6.5–12 kg (~6 months+), once baby has full head and neck control. Sessions of 20–45 minutes; not for naps.
  • Back carry — 9.5–15 kg (~12 months+).

One-handed Fidlock® magnetic buckles, ventilated back panel, foldable front panel with sunshade pocket, lumbar support, and adjustable seat width. Made from 51% nylon, 48% polyester, 1% polypropylene, with non-textile parts of natural rubber. Care: hand wash at 30°C, line dry — spot clean where you can.

How do I know it's safe? +

TUKO complies with CEN/TR 16512:2015, the European standard for baby carriers. Every carrier ships with a full printed manual and QR-linked fit videos at tuko.world/how, built around the T.I.C.K.S. babywearing checks: Tight, In view at all times, Close enough to kiss, Keep chin off the chest, Supported back.

For pre-term or low-birth-weight babies, or children with medical conditions, talk to a health professional before using any carrier — TUKO included.

What if it's not right? Returns & warranty. +

Returns: buy direct from tuko.world and you've got 30 days from delivery to change your mind, provided the carrier is unused and in its original condition. Refunds go out within 2 weeks of the carrier arriving back.

The Tuko Promise: a lifetime warranty on manufacturing defects — buckles, hardware, stitching, seams. If we built it wrong, we'll repair it, replace it, or refund it. Original purchaser, with proof of purchase. Your statutory rights sit on top of all of this, untouched.
